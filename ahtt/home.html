Introduction

Welcome to my website. I have some thoughts about technology that I'd like to share on the Internet, mainly just as a way of getting it written down. I'd post on Twitter but I don't think it's very intuitive for long-form discussion.

Website Q&A

  • Why does this look like it was made in the 90s?
    Because I can't be bothered to make nice CSS for this website. It's here for text content and nothing more.

  • Why is this website not using Wix/Wordpress/Whatever website builder is popular these days?
    I considered using Wordpress but found myself not particularly liking any .wordpress.com URLs. I could have, and it would have been easier, but I liked the idea of having it on my Github page anyway.

  • Do I care?
    No. I just like the idea of someone randomly stumbling across this on the Internet.

  • Can I make the website look nicer?
    I'll happily do the bare minimum to make the website functional but it's a push to be expecting anything particularly fancy. I imagine it'll look closer to a 90s webpage and I might lean heavily into that style.

  • How am I developing this website?
    Notepad.
